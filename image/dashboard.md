## 📊 Dashboard Preview

This dashboard is designed to provide a centralized view of business performance, structured into three main sections:

- **Left panel:** Navigation across business domains (Deposits, Credit, Indicators)
- **Center panel:** KPI monitoring, performance tracking, and analytical insights
- **Right panel:** Dynamic filters for time, region, and agency segmentation
